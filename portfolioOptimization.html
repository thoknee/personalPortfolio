Portfolio Optimizer

This project looks to create an efficient way to optimize portfolios based on what you're looking for. You can maximize the return, minimize the risk, look at a tradeoff between return/risk, and maximize the sharpe ratio. As always, the deployed version can be found here: Portfolio Optimizer
And the code can be found here: here

Set up

Upon opening the deployed site, you will be met with a side bar where your selections can be made. Upon choosing your tickers, start date, end date, and risk free rate, you can now choose your optimization and risk settings.

On the main site it will give you some general information about these tickers over the period of time selected. You can see the closing prices, the annual mean returns, the covariance, and the correlation between each stock and every other stock as follows:



From here, you can choose to optimize the portfolio in 4 different ways:

  1. Max Return: Finds the optimal weights to have the highest possible return over this period
  2. Minimum Variance: Optimal portfolio weights to find the smallest possible volatility.
  3. Risk/return tradeoff: Using a risk aversion factor, gamma, it finds the highest return and lowest volatility based on gamma. It is calculated by optimizing the following equation: return - gamma * volatility.
  4. Maximum Sharpe: Using the efficient frontier graph, it optimizes the sharpe ratio for these tickers.


Upon selecting one of the 4 optimization technique, the portfolio optimizer will give you the following information:
  1. Solution type: A simple line that shares that a solution was found and what exactly the solution was.
  2. Expected mean return, volatility, and sharpe of this optimization
  3. List of portfolio weights and a pie chart with visualization
  4. Backtest graph that illustrates the returns of this portfolio over this time
  5. Sharpe, Sortino, and Calmar ratios
  6. Graph of all the drawdowns as well as the maximum drawdown over this period
  7. Historical and parametric Value at Risk and conditional Value at Risk according to the risk metrics selected in the side bar.
  8. Monte Carlo simulation for this portfolio for selected amount of days
  9. If sharpe minimization is selected, a display of the efficient frontier will also be displayed.
